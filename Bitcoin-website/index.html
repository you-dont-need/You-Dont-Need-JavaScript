Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world. It uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority.
How do I buy Bitcoin?
You can buy Bitcoin through cryptocurrency exchanges, Bitcoin ATMs, or peer-to-peer platforms. Always ensure you're using a reputable service and follow security best practices.
