Spoiler Effect

Once upon a time in a land far, far away, there was a chicken named Cluck Norris. Cluck had a reputation for being the toughest chicken in the coop. One day, he challenged a fierce fox to a duel. Everyone gathered to watch the showdown. The fox was confident and said, "You’re just a chicken! What do you think you can do?"

Cluck replied, "Well, I’ve mastered the art of egg-fu!" The fox was puzzled but thought it was a joke. However, Cluck didn’t crack under pressure. He simply flapped his wings and did a chicken dance that was so mesmerizing, the fox fell asleep!

When the fox woke up, he found himself covered in feathers. Cluck had won! The moral of the story? Sometimes, it’s not about fighting but about dancing your way out of trouble. And if you ever find yourself in a tight spot, just remember: when in doubt, dance it out!
