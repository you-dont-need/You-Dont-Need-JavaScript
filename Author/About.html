Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015), fondly known as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India’s space and missile development programs and inspired millions with his vision for a developed nation. Beyond his scientific achievements, Dr. Kalam was admired as a teacher, author, and motivator who dedicated his life to inspiring the youth of India.

Books by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Wings of Fire

An autobiography of Dr. Kalam that chronicles his early life, struggles, and journey to becoming India’s top aerospace scientist and President.

Ignited Minds

This book inspires the youth of India to dream big and work towards transforming India into a developed nation.

India 2020

A vision document where Dr. Kalam outlines his roadmap for making India a developed nation by 2020 through science and technology.

Turning Points

A continuation of his autobiography, highlighting his journey from scientist to the President of India and the challenges he faced.
